Privacy Policy

LunaTime is a sleep calculator app in development by Potato Apps.

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Overview

This privacy policy applies to the LunaTime Android app made by Potato Apps. LunaTime is designed as a simple sleep calculator that helps users estimate sleep and wake-up times based on sleep cycles.

Information We Collect

LunaTime is currently in development. The planned app does not require accounts and is not designed to collect, store, sell, or share personal information.

Sleep Calculator Data

Any times users enter or calculate are intended to be used only inside the app on the user's own device. LunaTime does not need names, email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, payment information, or location data for its main sleep calculator feature.

Ads, Analytics, and Tracking

LunaTime is not designed to use advertising networks, tracking pixels, third-party tracking SDKs, user accounts, paywalls, or subscriptions.

Health Disclaimer

LunaTime provides general sleep timing suggestions based on sleep cycles. It is not a medical app and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Data Sharing

Potato Apps does not sell or share LunaTime user data with third parties. If app features change in the future, this policy will be updated before launch or release.

Children's Privacy

LunaTime is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if LunaTime features change. The date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent update.
