Privacy Policy

Status Saver is designed to work offline and does not collect personal data from users.

Last updated: May 30, 2026

Overview

This privacy policy applies to the Status Saver Android app made by Potato Apps. Status Saver does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information. The app is designed to work offline and does not send any user data to Potato Apps servers.

Information We Collect

Status Saver does not collect any of the following:

  • Name or email address
  • Phone number or contacts
  • Location data
  • Payment information
  • Device ID or advertising ID
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent externally
  • Advertising or tracking data

Offline Use

The app works offline for its core features. No user data is sent to Potato Apps servers. All media files handled by the app remain on the user's own device.

Ads, Analytics, and Tracking

Status Saver does not contain ads and does not use:

  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics tools or SDKs
  • Tracking pixels or third-party tracking SDKs
  • In-app purchases, paywalls, or subscriptions

App Permissions

The app may request Android permissions only when needed for its main features. For example, it may need access to selected local media or folders so it can display and save status files on the user's device. This access is used only on the user's device. Files are not uploaded, collected, stored, or shared by Potato Apps.

Data Sharing

Potato Apps does not share, sell, rent, or trade any user information with third parties. There is no user data to share.

Children's Privacy

Potato Apps does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the app does not collect any personal data at all, it does not store information from users of any age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if app features change. The date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent update. Continued use of the app after changes means acceptance of the updated policy.
