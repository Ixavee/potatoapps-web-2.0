Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how the Potato Apps website handles privacy.

Last updated: June 2, 2026

Overview

This privacy policy applies to the Potato Apps website. It does not replace the privacy policies for individual Potato Apps Android apps. Each app may have its own app-specific privacy policy.

Potato Apps is designed to keep things simple and private. The website does not require accounts, does not ask users to sign in, and does not sell personal information.

Information We Collect

The Potato Apps website does not directly collect personal information from visitors.

The website does not ask for:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Location data
  • Payment information
  • Account login details
  • Device ID or advertising ID

Accounts and Forms

The Potato Apps website does not require user accounts.

If the website includes a support email link and a visitor chooses to contact Potato Apps by email, the email address and message content will be visible to Potato Apps. This information is used only to read and reply to the message.

Cookies, Analytics, and Tracking

The Potato Apps website does not intentionally use:

  • Tracking cookies
  • Advertising pixels
  • Analytics tools
  • Third-party tracking SDKs
  • User profiling tools

External Links

The website may include links to external websites, such as Ko-fi or other support pages. When visitors open an external link, they leave the Potato Apps website. Those external websites have their own privacy policies and practices.

Potato Apps is not responsible for how external websites collect or handle information.

App-Specific Privacy Policies

This website may provide information about Potato Apps Android apps. Each app may have its own privacy policy explaining how that specific app handles data and permissions.

For example, the Status Saver app has its own privacy policy.

Status Saver Privacy Policy

Data Sharing

Potato Apps does not sell, rent, trade, or share personal information collected through this website.

Children's Privacy

The Potato Apps website is not designed to collect personal information from children. Potato Apps does not knowingly collect personal information from children through this website.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if the website changes. The date at the top of this page will show the most recent update.
